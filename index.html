What we monitor

Seven operational clocks. We watch the ones that are live for your property.

Not every property needs every category. The scan decides which are genuinely live — and KAIROS will tell you when there aren't enough to be worth watching.

01 Flood / FEMA

FEMA map revisions, zone and elevation changes, pending maps — anything that can reset flood-insurance rating before a carrier flags it.

02 Recertification / Structural

Milestone inspections, reserve studies, and special-assessment notices on older buildings — the costs that hit owners with little warning.

03 Insurance renewal

Carrier withdrawals, rate actions, and renewal windows in high-risk markets — surfaced ahead of the renewal, not at it.

04 Entity compliance

For LLC- and entity-owned property: registration status and annual-filing windows, so an ownership entity never lapses unnoticed.

05 Tax / assessment

Assessment shifts and appeal windows that feed insured-value adequacy and budgeting.

06 Ownership / transactions

New deeds, mortgages, and liens recorded against the property — and the obligations they can carry.

07 Use / occupancy

Whether actual use (such as short-term rental) matches how the property is insured — a gap that surfaces at claim time.

The discipline

A category only activates on verified facts. Most months, most stay stable — and we say so. That confirmed quiet is the work.
